# Deepseek OCR

Turn your images into text instantly with the power of artificial intelligence.

## What is Deepseek OCR?

Deepseek OCR is a smart tool that reads text from your images, just like a human would. Whether you have a photo of a document, a screenshot, a receipt, or any image containing text, this tool can extract all the text content for you automatically.

Think of it as having a personal assistant who can look at any image and type out all the text they see - but faster and more accurate.

## What Can You Do With It?

### Extract Text from Documents

Have a photo of a paper document? Deepseek OCR can read it and give you all the text in an editable format. Perfect for:

- Digitizing old paper documents
- Converting photos of notes into text
- Extracting information from receipts and invoices
- Reading text from scanned pages

### Convert Screenshots to Text

Captured a screenshot with important information? Extract all the text content instantly:

- Pull text from website screenshots
- Get text from application interfaces
- Convert image-based slides to editable content

### Preserve Formatting

The tool doesn't just extract plain text - it understands the structure of your document and can preserve formatting like headings, lists, and paragraphs.

## How It Works for You

Using Deepseek OCR is simple:

1. **Select Your Image** - Choose any image file from your computer (supports JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and BMP formats)

2. **Let the AI Read It** - The intelligent vision system analyzes your image and identifies all text content

3. **Get Your Text** - Receive clean, accurate text that you can copy, edit, or use however you need

## Key Features

### High Accuracy

Uses advanced vision intelligence to accurately recognize text, even in challenging situations like:

- Handwritten notes
- Poor quality images
- Complex layouts
- Multiple languages

### Flexible Output

The text extraction adapts to your needs. By default, it converts documents to well-formatted markdown, making it easy to use the text in various applications.

### Custom Instructions

Want the text in a specific format? You can provide custom instructions to tell the tool exactly how you want your text extracted and formatted.

### Smart Image Understanding

The tool automatically detects your image type and optimizes the text extraction process accordingly, ensuring the best possible results.
